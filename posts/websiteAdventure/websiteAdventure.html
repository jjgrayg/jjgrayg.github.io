I decided to reactivate my CodeCademy subscription and take a look at what sort of classes they offer for website design and implementation. I found they had a Skill Path exactly for that called "Build a Website with HTML, CSS, and Github Pages" (mind you this is not an advertisement for the service, just the path I took). I got about halfway through the course before I decided to try to make my own website. I felt I had enough of a loose grasp on the languages HTML and CSS to try to build my own, personal, website and take note of the challenges I faced along the way.
