About Me

As you've probably gleamed from the website thus far, my name is Jarod Graygo and I am a student at Kent State University in Kent, OH currently pursuing my Bachelor of Science degree in Computer Science. Some of the things I'm most interested in are:

  • Computers (obviously)
  • The wacky parts of physics
  • Music
  • Video games
  • Puzzles

In reference to coding, though, the language I'm most fluent in is C++, but the first language I ever learned was Java when I was 12, which I only learned so that I could mod Minecraft. I know a decent bit of Python (I've written a couple projects in the language), and I am currently working on learning HTML5, CSS, and JavaScript. While I am by no means an expert on any topic I discuss here, I still think taking the time to write something about an interesting topic will allow me to gain a deeper understanding of it. I'll have to do more in-depth research about a topic than a simple glance at the first headline I see. If you catch any errors in anything I write (be it a post or some code that can be better), send an email to the one listed on my contact page and I'll fix it up, and learn a bit more at the same time!
